About Me

Overview


Hi, I am Sally Elizabeth Mitchell, I am from Positano, Italy , I am a third-year student at Arizona State University's New College of Interdisciplinary Arts and Sciences, where I am pursuing a degree in Biology (BS) and a minor in Communication. I am the recipient of an NCUIRE research scholarship, which has allowed me to engage in research on river pollution, working alongside one of my professors. In My studies, I am focusing on biodiversity and sustainable practices while also furthering my knowledge of endangered species and marine ecosystems. My interest in communication stems from her goal of communicating the importance of conservation to a large audience. I have also worked as an intern at the Phoenix Zoo for the last two years. Upon completion of my undergraduate studies, I intend to apply to graduate school to further my knowledge of biology and progress toward a career as a researcher.
